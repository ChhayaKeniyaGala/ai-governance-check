AI governance quick check

Two minutes. Rate one AI use case, then your organization. Amber marks a risk or a gap; green marks you're covered.

Part 1 · One AI use case — how risky is it?
Does it make or heavily influence decisions about people (hiring, lending, performance, eligibility)?
Does it touch customer or personal data?
Does it act automatically, without a human reviewing each output?
Does it affect money, safety, or legal outcomes?
Is it a vendor feature or tool you didn't build or fully vet?
Part 2 · Your organization — are you ready to govern it?
Do you have an up-to-date inventory of AI in use across the org?
Does each significant AI use have a named owner?
Is someone reviewing AI outputs before they're trusted?
Do you review the AI built into vendor tools you buy?
Do you have visibility into what your AI usage costs?
